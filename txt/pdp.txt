Felix Arvid Ulf Kjellberg known online as PewDiePie, is a Swedish YouTuber, comedian and video game commentator, formerly best known for his Let's Play commentaries and now mostly known for his comedic formatted shows.
On 15 August 2013, PewDiePie became the most-subscribed user on YouTube, being briefly surpassed in late 2013 by YouTube Spotlight. After regaining the top position on 23 December 2013 the channel has now amassed over 93 million subscribers as of April 2019, although PewDiePie has lost his first rank on Youtube several times to T-Series for short time intervals.
