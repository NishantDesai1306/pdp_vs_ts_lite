T-Series is an Indian music record label and film production company founded by Gulshan Kumar in 1983. It is primarily known for Bollywood music soundtracks and Indi-pop music. T-Series is one of the largest Indian music record labels, along with Zee Music and Sony Music India.
The T-Series YouTube channel, run by a small team of 13 people, primarily shows music videos and occasionally film trailers. It is the most-viewed YouTube channel, with over 56 billion views as of 19 December 2018. With over 92 million subscribers as of April 2019, it also ranks as the second most-subscribed channel behind PewDiePie.
